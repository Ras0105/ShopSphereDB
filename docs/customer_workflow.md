## Customer Workflow (Final)

```text
Customer opens website
        ↓
Browse/Search/Filter Products
        ↓
View Product Details
        ↓
Add to Cart
        ↓
Login/Register (if required)
        ↓
Modify Cart or Continue Shopping
        ↓
Proceed to Checkout
        ↓
Select/Add Shipping Address
        ↓
Validate Inventory
        ↓
Make Payment
        ↓
Payment Success?
      /       \
    No         Yes
    ↓           ↓
Retry      Order Confirmed
Payment         ↓
          Inventory Updated
                 ↓
              Packed
                 ↓
              Shipped
                 ↓
          Out for Delivery
                 ↓
              Delivered
                 ↓
        Review Product (Optional)
                 ↓
      Return/Refund (Optional)
```
