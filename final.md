## Customer Workflow (Final)

```text
Customer opens website
        ↓
Browse/Search/Filter Products
        ↓
View Product Details
        ↓
Add to Cart
        ↓
Login/Register (if required)
        ↓
Modify Cart or Continue Shopping
        ↓
Proceed to Checkout
        ↓
Select/Add Shipping Address
        ↓
Validate Inventory
        ↓
Make Payment
        ↓
Payment Success?
      /       \
    No         Yes
    ↓           ↓
Retry      Order Confirmed
Payment         ↓
          Inventory Updated
                 ↓
              Packed
                 ↓
              Shipped
                 ↓
          Out for Delivery
                 ↓
              Delivered
                 ↓
        Review Product (Optional)
                 ↓
      Return/Refund (Optional)
```

## Admin Workflow (final)
# ShopSphere – Admin Workflow

```text
Admin opens Admin Panel
        ↓
Admin Login
        ↓
Dashboard
        ↓
Manage Categories
(Add / Update / Delete Categories)
        ↓
Manage Products
(Add / Update / Delete Products)
        ↓
Manage Inventory
(Update Stock / Reorder Stock)
        ↓
Manage Orders
(View New Orders)
        ↓
Verify Payment Status
        ↓
Process Orders
        ↓
Update Order Status
Pending
   ↓
Packed
   ↓
Shipped
   ↓
Out for Delivery
   ↓
Delivered
        ↓
Handle Returns/Refunds (Optional)
        ↓
View Reviews & Customer Feedback
        ↓
Generate Reports & Analytics
(Sales, Revenue, Low Stock, Top Products)
```
